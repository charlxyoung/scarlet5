Scarlet

a pro-apocalyptic story set in ancient Kikiyuland of Central Kenya

THE
IS COMING SOON

Those who throng to watch Denka's performance during the "One Bone Dance" competition at the annual Ngwiko Rite of Passage Ceremony in Virgin Land, have no inkling that her lips bear the tinder that would spark off the Day of Scarlet; the day for revealing of the fate of Good and Evil.

Scarlet, a pro-apocalyptic story set in ancient Kikiyuland of Central Kenya, reennacts the recurrent powerplay amongst principalities and the attendant consequences on mankind.

MEET THE AUTHOR

Alexander Emmanuel Ochogwu is a strategist and prose-fiction writer who loves to tread uncharted paths through his writings. He is the author of Omo, and The Diary of a Boy Soldier series. Alexander lives in Abuja and Port Harcourt, Nigeria.
